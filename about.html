About Us

Who We Are:

We are a Christian ministry, not sponsored by or affiliated with any particular church, denomination, or other entity. For more info, please see our Statement of Faith.

Our Mission:

It is the mission of Mumbwe Sunday School to provide sound Biblical resources so the Good News of the Bible, leading to Salvation and encouraging spiritual growth, can be shared with children around the world.

Our Resources:

We are committed to helping dedicated teachers like you share the Good News of Jesus with kids by creating meaningful and fun-filled Sunday school materials. We provide complete lesson plans along with ideas for bulletin boards, crafts, object talks, games, skits, stories, songs, teaching tips, and much more. We also offer our unique Topical Index, which allows you to quickly find ideas for specific subjects and themes. To learn more about the benefits of web-based resources, see: Why Choose an Online Curriculum? and Which Online Curriculum is Best for You?

How We Got Started:

Mumbwe Sunday School Place began with three friends, who all happened to be volunteer Sunday school teachers. Discussion of creating this site began in the summer of 1999. For more information on how we got started and how we grew into a leading web-based Sunday school resource, see: Site History.

Our Backgrounds:

We have a wide variety of educational backgrounds and teaching experience. Some of us served as children's pastors or teachers. Others worked on mission fields around the world. Members of our staff also attended workshops, classes, and seminars relating to children's ministry. Combined, we have several decades of Sunday school teaching experience. For more information, see: Our Staff.

We are always looking for ways to improve this website. We encourage questions and suggestions to help us further enhance our resources. Please let us know if you discover any areas for improvement, and thank you for visiting!
