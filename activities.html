Sunday School Activities

Role Playing

Role playing is an engaging way for children to bring Bible stories to life. Kids can act out scenes from the Bible, portraying key characters and reliving the stories in a fun and memorable way. It's an interactive way to learn while using creativity and teamwork. This activity helps children understand biblical stories better and can be done as a group or in pairs. You can incorporate costumes, props, or simple DIY items to make the activity more enjoyable!
